# User Guide

Quick guide on how the Loan and Payment features work once they're up and running.

## Loans

To see a student's loans, go to the student table and click the **Loans** button next to their name. This takes you to that student's loan page.

To add a new loan, there should be a form where you fill in:
- Amount (how much the loan is for)
- Loan Type - pick between Tuition, Books, or Living Expenses
- Status - Pending, Approved, or Disbursed

Once submitted it should show up in that student's loan list. Clicking **View** shows all the loans for that specific student only (not everyone else's).

## Payments

Same idea but for payments. From a loan's page, click **Payments** to see all the payments made toward that loan.

To log a payment, fill in:
- Payment Amount
- Payment Date
- Payment Method - Cash, Bank Transfer, or Online Payment

The payment list should also show the Total Paid so far and how much is left (Remaining Balance = loan amount - total paid).
